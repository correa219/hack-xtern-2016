Frequently Asked Questions

What's HackXtern?

HackXtern is an eight hour hackathon for K-12 kids! Come hack, learn and have fun! We will have mentors to answer your questions and help you through the process!

When/where is it?

Saturday, July 23rd 2016, starting at 9:00am. The event will be held at the Ivy Tech Community College Corporate College and Culinary Center, 2820 North Meridian Street, Indianapolis, IN 46208

The closest parking is easily accessed on 28th between Meridian and Illinois. Check out this map for info on where!

I have younger kids, not sure if mine can stay that long. Can I leave early?

Sure! Check our schedule, stay as long as you want. Do keep in mind that if you are going for prizes it will affect your participation.

Will there be a limited number of participants?

Unfortunately, yes. We will only be accepting 60 students.

How will application process work?

Most applicants will be come first, serve first. However, this is not a guarantee.

What if my plans change and I can't come?

Notify us as soon as possible so we can open spaces for waitlisted people!

I don't have a teammate, is that okay?

Yes! We will pair you up if needed, but you can ride solo too!

How big are teams?

One or two, please!

How much does it cost to attend?

It's free! However, transportation to the event and back is not provided.

This will be my first hackathon, is that okay?

Yes! There is no better time to start and learn than now! The awesome Xterns will be here to mentor you and help you build something awesome.

How much programming experience do I need?

None, all levels of experience are welcome.

Will food be provided?

Yes, we will give you lunch!

Do I need to bring a laptop?

We will be using Chromebooks, but you are free to bring your own device!

What can I do?

It's open ended! You can do a website, a game, a mobile application, whatever you can within our time constraint!

I am already a super awesome programmer, do I need a mentor?

Only if you want! You can do your own thing and we can help you if needed!

Are parents welcome to stay or just drop off the kids?

You can do either. Please keep in mind that the computer labs might not have enough space. However, we will have a general area for you.

How are presentations going to work?

Every team will get to present! It will be science fair style. So parents and judges can walk around to see everyone to choose winners.

Can we see others' presentations?

It depends! Presentations will work differently, it will be science fair style. So, one of you can present and the other one can go look around other teams work.
